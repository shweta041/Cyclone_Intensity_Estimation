About Us

Cyclone Intensity and Distance Estimation is a dedicated team of meteorologists, data scientists, and environmental enthusiasts committed to advancing cyclone prediction. With expertise in meteorology, data analytics, and disaster management, our mission is to provide accurate and timely information to help communities prepare for cyclones. We harness technology and data-driven insights to enhance forecasting, mitigate risks, and protect vulnerable communities. Using cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and satellite imagery, we develop predictive models for cyclone intensity and track. Our analyses of historical and real-time data enable us to offer precise forecasts and insights to governments, disaster agencies, and at-risk communities.
